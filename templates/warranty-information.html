5. WHAT IS NOT COVERED

WARRANTY EXCLUSIONS

The following items are NOT covered under warranty:

Normal Wear and Tear

  • Cosmetic damage from normal use
  • Wear on moving parts and surfaces
  • Fading or discoloration of materials
  • Minor scratches and dents

Improper Use or Maintenance

  • Damage from improper operation
  • Lack of routine maintenance
  • Unauthorized modifications
  • Use beyond intended capacity

External Factors

  • Damage from accidents or collisions
  • Weather-related damage (storms, hail, etc.)
  • Damage from pests or animals
  • Damage from improper storage or transport

Third-Party Components

  • Appliances (covered by manufacturer warranty)
  • Electronics and entertainment systems
  • Aftermarket accessories or modifications
  • Custom installations by third parties
6. WARRANTY CLAIM PROCESS

How to File a Warranty Claim

  1. Contact Us: Call our warranty hotline or submit online
  2. Describe the Issue: Provide detailed description of the problem
  3. Provide Information: Order number, serial number, photos if possible
  4. Schedule Inspection: We'll arrange for inspection or service
  5. Repair Authorization: We'll provide repair estimate and timeline
  6. Service Completion: Repairs completed and verified

Required Information for Claims

  • Order number and purchase date
  • Vehicle serial number
  • Detailed description of the issue
  • Photos or videos of the problem
  • Contact information for scheduling

7. MAINTENANCE REQUIREMENTS

Required Maintenance Schedule

  • Monthly: Check for leaks, inspect seals, test systems
  • Quarterly: Deep cleaning, system checks, filter changes
  • Annually: Professional inspection, system maintenance
  • As Needed: Address any issues immediately

Maintenance Documentation

Keep records of all maintenance performed, including:

  • Date and type of maintenance
  • Service provider information
  • Parts replaced or repaired
  • Receipts and invoices

IMPORTANT: Failure to perform required maintenance may void warranty coverage. Keep all maintenance records for warranty claims.
