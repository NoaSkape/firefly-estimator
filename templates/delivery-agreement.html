5. DELIVERY REQUIREMENTS

IMPORTANT DELIVERY REQUIREMENTS

The buyer must ensure the following requirements are met before delivery:

Site Preparation

  • Clear access path for delivery vehicle (minimum 12 feet wide)
  • Level ground for placement (within 6 inches of level)
  • No overhead obstructions (minimum 14 feet clearance)
  • Stable ground that can support the weight of the vehicle
  • Proper drainage to prevent water accumulation

Utility Connections

  • Electrical hookup available (if applicable)
  • Water connection available (if applicable)
  • Sewer/septic connection available (if applicable)
  • All connections must meet local building codes

Permits and Regulations

  • All necessary permits obtained by buyer
  • Compliance with local zoning regulations
  • Compliance with HOA rules (if applicable)
  • Proper insurance coverage in place
6. DELIVERY PROCESS

Delivery Day Process

  1. Delivery team will contact buyer 1-2 hours before arrival
  2. Buyer or designated representative must be present
  3. Final inspection will be conducted upon arrival
  4. Vehicle will be positioned as directed by buyer
  5. Basic setup and leveling will be performed
  6. Buyer will sign delivery acceptance form

What's Included in Delivery

  • Transportation to delivery site
  • Basic positioning and leveling
  • Removal of transport materials
  • Basic walkthrough of vehicle features
  • Delivery acceptance documentation

What's NOT Included in Delivery

  • Site preparation or modifications
  • Utility connections or hookups
  • Permit acquisition or compliance
  • Extended setup or customization
  • Return transportation
